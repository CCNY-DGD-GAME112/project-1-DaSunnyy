---READ ME---
ZOMBIE MASHER: By Daniel Garcia

---PREMISE---
Zombie-esque Monsters are running amok in the city and causing chaos! 
This place isn't safe anymore... Make your way to the shelter towards the outskirts of the city! SURVIVE AT ALL COSTS!

---WIN CONDITION--- Make it to the end of the city and escape the Zombie Apocalypse
---LOSE CONDITION 1--- Health reaches 0
---LOSE CONDITION 2--- Zombification Timer reaches 0

---ITEMS---
Food: Restores 2 Health
Medical Kit: Full Restores Health
Antidote: Cures / Stops Zombification Process
Gun: Allows you to shoot bullets with the SPACEBAR Key
Ammunition: Refills Ammo to continue Shooting

---CONTROLS & STATS---
Maximum Health: 8 (Can Heal up with Food / Medical Kit)
Maximum Ammo: 6 (Can be reloaded with Ammunition)
A Key: Move Left
D Key: Move Right
W Key: Climb Up Ladders / Aim up with Gun
S Key: Climb Down Ladders
J Key: Run
K Key: Jump
L Key: Hammer Swing Attack (Deals 1 Damage / Can be Spammed)
Spacebar Key: Shoot (Must acquire Gun First! / Deals 5 Damage)

---ZOMBIES---
Maximum Health: 5
Swing Attack: 1 Damage
Bite Attack: 2 Damage (Infects with Zombification if it connects!)

Bite attacks are deadly; not only do they hurt more than a swing attack, but they'll also infect you!
If you don't find an antidote to stop the zombification process (25 seconds), you'll turn into a zombie!
No matter how much health you have, you're DONE!

Beware, as some Zombies have been seen to be colossal in size! Who knows what they're capable of...
